This project started as a personal challenge to implement a physically accurate collision simulator from scratch — no physics libraries, no shortcuts. The derivations took nearly 5 pages of hand-written equations over several days. The engine uses an event-driven approach: rather than stepping through time at fixed intervals, it calculates the exact moment of the next collision and jumps to it. Currently 1D, with a full 2D extension in progress.
